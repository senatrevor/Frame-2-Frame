"Frame-2-Frame" is all about situations and our ability to adapt to them.

Inspired by old animation techniques, I created several “cel sheets” and various “background plates” for them to be placed over.

How you choose to place them is up to you.

I believe we have the ability to inject who we are and what we stand for in order to shape the world around us.

Whether you decide to stay put or explore, your world is what you make it out to be.
